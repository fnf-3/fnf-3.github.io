About FNF Spaghetti

FNF Spaghetti is a Friday Night Funkin' mod featuring LE SSERAFIM, bringing K-pop rhythm battles to your browser for free with addictive note-hitting gameplay.

How to Play FNF Spaghetti

You step into a rap battle against LE SSERAFIM members. Colored arrows scroll up the screen. Press ↑ ↓ ← → or W A S D exactly when the arrows hit the static markers at the top.

Hit notes to fill your health bar. Miss notes and your health drops. Let it hit zero and you lose. Choose from Easy, Normal, or Hard difficulty. Start with Easy to learn the beat, then work your way up.

Long combos mean bigger scores. The longer you stay perfect, the higher your multiplier. Your progress saves automatically in your browser. Come back anytime and your high scores are still there waiting.

Controls

  • Main keys ↑ ↓ ← →
  • Alt keys W A S D
  • Select song Enter
  • Pause ESC
  • Volume + and –

FNF Spaghetti blends classic rhythm gameplay with Kpop energy. Easy to learn, hard to put down. Free, browser-based, and packed with LE SSERAFIM style. Jump in and hit those notes.
