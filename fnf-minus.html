About FNF Minus

FNF Minus is an FNF mod that redesigns every character based on their health bar icons, bringing a completely fresh look alongside familiar melodies – now faster, harder, and more challenging than ever.

How to Play FNF Minus

You still step into Boyfriend's shoes, battling to win Girlfriend's heart. But this time, Daddy Dearest, Pico, Skid, and Pump all rock new looks – directly inspired by their health bar icons. Familiar yet strange. Cute yet unpredictable.

Press ↑ ↓ ← → exactly when the arrows hit the markers. The songs are the same tracks you know, but remixed with faster tempos and tighter patterns. No room for slacking. Every keystroke counts. Every combo must be held.

The Minus art style transforms the entire game space. Colors, layouts, visuals – everything is refreshed. This rhythm party never stops dancing.

Signature Features

  • Brand new character designs based on health bar icons
  • Familiar songs with increased speed and difficulty
  • Unique Minus graphic style, fresh from start to finish

Controls

  • Hit notes: ↑ ↓ ← →
  • Hold long notes: Press and hold corresponding key
  • Select song: Enter
  • Pause: ESC
  • Volume: + / –

FNF Minus is a gift for Friday Night Funkin' fans craving something fresh. Creatively redesigned characters, familiar tracks turned into real challenges. Jump in, find the beat, and prove you're not just familiar with the classics – you've got what it takes to master this brand new remixed world.
