About FNF

Test your rhythm skills in Friday Night Funkin'! Battle through intense rap duels, press arrow keys in sync with the beat to win your girlfriend's heart. Play the legendary indie rhythm game now!

Let the Music Lead – Are You Ready to Become a Rap Legend?

Friday Night Funkin' is more than just a rhythm game. It's a street battle where every note is an attack, every drumbeat is a chance to prove yourself. You step into the shoes of Boyfriend, the blue-haired young man determined to win Girlfriend's heart through musical talent. But the journey is far from easy as you face off against a roster of fierce opponents, from an overprotective father to mysterious beat masters.

How to Play FNF: Easy to Learn, Hard to Master

Every stage in Friday Night Funkin' is a life-or-death rap duel between you and your opponent. Your mission is straightforward:

  • Watch the scrolling arrows moving across the screen from right to left
  • Press the corresponding arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) on your keyboard with precision
  • Keep your health bar from depleting – every missed note gives your rival the upper hand
  • Complete the song with high accuracy to claim victory

Pro Tips for Beginners

  • Start with Easy mode to get familiar with each song's rhythm pattern
  • Focus on repeating note sequences – most FNF tracks have predictable loops
  • Keep your eyes on the hit zone instead of scanning the whole screen
  • Feel the groove, don't just rely on sight – let your ears guide your fingers

FAQs

What are the controls for Friday Night Funkin'?

  • Use the ↑ ↓ ← → arrow keys to match the scrolling notes. That's it – simple to learn, challenging to perfect.

How many weeks are in the base game?

  • The original version features 7 main weeks, each with 3 songs and unique opponents, plus bonus weeks added through updates.

Can I play FNF with custom songs?

  • Absolutely! Mods allow you to add thousands of fan made tracks, from original compositions to covers of popular hits across all genres.
